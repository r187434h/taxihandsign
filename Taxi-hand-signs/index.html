About Taxi Hand Signals.
It'll blow your mind.

Taxi hand signals are a unique and essential part of South Africa's public transportation culture. They allow commuters to communicate their destination quickly and efficiently to minibus taxi drivers without saying a word.

These signals are not just practical; they also reflect the country's vibrant urban life and community connections. Understanding them helps both locals and visitors navigate the busy taxi routes safely and confidently.

Why are they important? Knowing taxi hand signals improves commuter safety, reduces misunderstandings, and supports the smooth flow of transport across cities and townships.

Learn the signals, respect the etiquette, and become part of South Africa's iconic transport culture!
